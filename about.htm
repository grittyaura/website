about me



Aura Moreno is a Dominican artist, director, DJ, designer, and overall creative force whose work moves literally and emotionally. Whether it’s dembow, jungle, hip-hop, or house, every song is made with intention: to dance, to heal, to reflect. Born in Providence, RI, and shaped by chapters in New York, Paris, Santo Domingo, LA, Paris, and London, Aura has built her own creative universe blurring the lines between music, visuals, and design with unapologetic freedom. She writes, directs, and edits everything herself. Bold. Raw. Playful. Following her London debut with Aura’s Greatest Hits on NTS Radio, her sonic rebellion have continued to draw attention from Paper, Dazed, Office, and Remezcla. Beyond music, Aura is a respected thought leader having led artist talks at Brown University on identity, creativity, and cultural transformation. With every release, visual, and performance, Aura moves with fearless purpose, shaping culture and leaving an undeniable and lasting mark.
