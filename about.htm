About me:

Hailing from Providence, RI, musician and multi-hyphenate artist Aura Moreno, formerly known as Iris Creamer, tells honest and vulnerable stories; shared with intention to dance, heal, and reflect with. Truly genre-less, Aura’s music ranges from house to rap, acoustic to trip-hop, from truth to fantasy, and english to spanish. Her multidisciplinary art forms blend seamlessly between singer-writer, song production, digital art, film, mixed media, collage, and fashion design. Creation upon creation, she builds her thoroughly unique and colorful DIY empire.
